Answers to Frequently Asked Questions

What material can be applied to Vodunaks ?

Superhydrophobic spray is suitable for suede, nubuck and textiles. Also Vodunax can be used for un-lacquered skin, but the effect of use will be weaker due to the high density of the material. To test the effect of the spray, it is recommended to spray the product on a small, barely visible area of ​​the material. How long will the effect last after treatment with the super hydrophobic Vodunax?

How long will the effect last after treatment with the super hydrophobic Vodunax?

The effect of the action and its duration depends on the structure of the material to which the spray is sprayed, as well as the amount of the agent applied and the frequency of operation of the footwear. When applying a spray on suede and nubuck - a porous and soft material, the effect will last about two months. If you use a hydrophobic spray on the skin - a more durable and less porous material, the duration of the effect can reach 20-30 days.

After applying the spray to the surface of the shoe, it is not recommended to physically work on it: wash, scratch or rub to avoid weakening the effect of the spray. However, during the research it was proved that even with the weakening effect of the Vodunax spray, the footwear will be resistant to wetting for several more months.

How much does one bottle of Vodunax spray suffice?

The volume of the spray bottle is 100 milliliters. The consumption of the product depends on the structure of the material and the size of the footwear. When applying the product to textile or other highly absorbent surface, one bottle is enough for 50 square cm.

On average, one bottle is enough to process 5 pairs of seasonal shoes, which allows you to keep the dryness of feet and the beauty of shoes for the whole family.

What are the guarantees that Vodunax will keep the cleanliness and dryness of your shoes?

Properly coated shoes are protected from dirt and water for several months by 100%. This result is guaranteed due to the unique nano-hydrophobic composition of the spray. Vodunax does not contain toxic components, wax, acrylic, silicone, oil and paraffin, which makes the product absolutely safe for the footwear surface. Super hydrophobic spray creates an invisible transparent barrier between the material and moisture, preserving the original color of your shoes.
